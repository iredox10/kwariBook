# KwariBook User Manual | Littafin Jagoran KwariBook

Welcome to **KwariBook**, the most advanced digital bookkeeping system tailored specifically for the textile traders of Kantin Kwari.
Barka da zuwa **KwariBook**, manhajar lissafi mafi inganci da aka samar musamman domin yan kasuwar atamfobi da shadda na Kantin Kwari.

---

## 1. Getting Started | Matakan Farko

### **Login | Shiga Manhajarta**
*   **English:** You can log in using your **Phone Number** (OTP) or **Email** (Magic Link). This ensures your data is backed up to the cloud.
*   **Hausa:** Kuna iya shiga ta amfani da **Lambar Wayarku** (OTP) ko **Email**. Wannan yana tabbatar da cewa bayanan ku suna adana a yanar gizo (cloud).

### **Setting Up Your Shop | Seta Shago**
*   **English:** Go to **Settings** to add your shop's name, logo, and detailed location (Building, Block, Floor, and Landmark).
*   **Hausa:** Je zuwa **Saituna (Settings)** domin saka sunan shago, hoto (logo), da adireshin shagonku daki-daki (Suna gida, Block, hawa nawa, da wani sanannen wuri na kusa).

---

## 2. Sales & Anti-Scam | Sayarwa da Kariya daga Masu Algungumi

### **Recording a Sale | Rikodin Ciniki**
*   **English:** Select a shop, add products from inventory, and choose the customer. Use the **Mic icon** to speak names in Hausa.
*   **Hausa:** Zabi shago, saka kayan da aka saya, sannan zabi mai saya. Kuna iya amfani da **hoton Mic** domin fadar suna da baki.

### **Fake Alert Protection | Kariyar Karya (Fake Alert)**
*   **English:** When a customer pays via transfer, a **Security Checklist** appears. You must verify the actual bank balance and sender name before finalizing.
*   **Hausa:** Idan abokin ciniki ya biya ta transfer, manhajar za ta nuna muku **Jerin Dubawa (Checklist)**. Dole ne ku duba ragowar kudin bankinku da sunan wanda ya turo kafin ku kammala cinikin.

### **Scam Alert | Sanarwar Algungumi**
*   **English:** If a customerâ€™s phone number has been flagged for fake alerts by other traders, a **Red Warning** will appear instantly.
*   **Hausa:** Idan lambar wayar abokin ciniki tana cikin jerin wadanda aka taba kamawa da tura sakon bogi (fake alert), manhajar za ta nuna muku **Gargadi ja** nan take.

## 3. Inventory & Remnants | Kayan Shago da Rage-rage

### **Smart Units | Ma'aunin Kaya**
*   **English:** Track goods in Bundles, Bales, Suits, Yards, or Meters.
*   **Hausa:** Kuna iya lissafa kaya ta Bundle, Bale, Suit, Yadi, ko Mita.

### **Remnants (Rage-rage) | Rage-rage**
*   **English:** When you cut a bundle, the app asks for the leftover yards. These are saved automatically as "Remnants" with a 20% discount.
*   **Hausa:** Idan kuka yanka bundle, manhajar za ta tambaye ku ko akwai rage-rage. Ana adana su daban tare da ragi (discount) na kashi 20%.

### **Flash Sale | Saukin Farashi**
*   **English:** Use the **Flash Sale** button in the inventory tab to apply a mass discount to all remnants to clear space.
*   **Hausa:** Kuna iya amfani da maballin **Flash Sale** domin yin saukin farashi ga dukkan rage-rage dake shago lokaci guda.

---

## 4. Credit (Bashi) & Customers | Bashi da Masu Sura

### **Credit Scores | Taurarin Aminci**
*   **English:** Customers are ranked 1 to 5 stars based on how fast they pay back. Check stars before giving "Salo."
*   **Hausa:** Manhajar tana ba abokan ciniki tauraro 1 zuwa 5 dangane da yadda suke biyan bashi. Duba taurari kafin ka ba mutum "Salo."

### **Partial Payments | Biyan Bashi Kashi-kashi**
*   **English:** Record payments in small amounts. The **Progress Bar** shows how much balance is remaining.
*   **Hausa:** Kuna iya karbar kudin bashi kashi-kashi. **Layin Nuna Nasara (Progress Bar)** zai nuna muku nawa aka biya da nawa ya rage.

---

## 5. Finances & Reports | Rahotannin Kudi

### **Net Profit | Ribar Da Ta Rage**
*   **English:** The Reports tab shows your real take-home profit by calculating: `Sales - Cost of Goods - Expenses`.
*   **Hausa:** Bangaren rahotanni yana nuna muku asalin ribar da ta rage muku bayan an cire kudin kaya da sauran kashe-kashe.

### **End of Day | Karshen Rana**
*   **English:** Check the dashboard at 6:00 PM to see exactly how much cash should be in your pocket.
*   **Hausa:** Duba dashboard a karshen rana domin sanin ainihin adadin kudin da ya kamata ya kasance a aljihunku.

### **Bank Statement | Takardar Banki**
*   **English:** Generate a professional **PDF Statement** to show banks your turnover for loan applications.
*   **Hausa:** Kuna iya fitar da **Takardar PDF** mai nuna tarihin kasuwancinku domin kaiwa banki idan kuna neman bashi.

---

## 6. Privacy & Staff | Sirri da Yara Maza

### **Alhaji Mode | Yanayin Alhaji**
*   **English:** Tap the **Eye icon** to hide all money amounts. Shop boys can record sales without seeing your total balance or profit.
*   **Hausa:** Taba **hoton Ido** domin boye dukkan lambobin kudi. Yara maza za su iya rikodin ciniki ba tare da sun ga jumullar kudin ku ko ribarku ba.

---

## 7. Branding & Tools | Tallata Shago da Wasu Injinai

### **Professional Receipts | Rasit Mai Suna**
*   **English:** Share a high-quality image receipt on WhatsApp containing your logo, address, and navigation landmarks.
*   **Hausa:** Aika rasit mai hoto da sunan shago da tambari (logo) ga abokin ciniki ta WhatsApp.

### **Digital Business Card | Katin Shago na Zamani**
*   **English:** Generate a beautiful digital card with your shop details and latest patterns to share with new customers.
*   **Hausa:** Fitar da kyakkyawan katin shago na zamani mai nuna wurin shagonku da sabbin samfuran da kuka kawo.

### **Barcode Printing | Barcode**
*   **English:** Generate and print barcodes for your bales to make scanning and selling 10x faster.
*   **Hausa:** Samar da barcode domin manawa a jikin bale domin yin ciniki cikin sauri.
